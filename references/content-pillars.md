# Content Pillar Planning (Topic Scaler)

Goal: Build unmatched topical authority with 5 pillars and 50 subpillars.

## Why Pillar Structure Matters

Google rewards topical authority. A site that covers ONE topic deeply from every angle ranks better than a site with scattered content on many topics. The pillar-cluster model signals to Google: "This site is the expert on [topic]."

## The 5-Pillar Framework

### Step 1: Define 5 Main Pillars

Each pillar should be:
- Broad enough to support 10 subpillar articles
- Narrow enough to be clearly topically related
- Directly relevant to your business or audience

Example for a Podcast Niche:
- Pillar 1: Podcast Equipment
- Pillar 2: Podcast Recording & Production
- Pillar 3: Podcast Growth & Marketing
- Pillar 4: Podcast Monetization
- Pillar 5: Podcast Hosting & Distribution

### Step 2: Create 10 Subpillars Per Pillar (50 total)

Each subpillar is a standalone article that:
- Covers one specific aspect of the main pillar
- Links back to the main pillar page
- Links to 2-3 related subpillars
- Targets a medium-competition keyword

For each subpillar, define:
- Target keyword (primary)
- Search intent
- 3 secondary keywords
- Recommended word count
- Key questions to answer

### Step 3: Build the Pillar Hub Page

The main pillar page should:
- Be 3,000-5,000 words
- Cover the topic at a high level
- Link out to ALL 10 subpillar articles
- Target the head term for the pillar
- Serve as the "ultimate guide" to the topic

### Step 4: Map Internal Link Flow

Draw the authority flow:
- Homepage → Pillar pages (most authority)
- Pillar pages → Subpillar articles
- Subpillar articles → Other subpillars (cross-links)
- All articles → Pillar page (back-links)

## Output: Complete Pillar Plan

Deliver a structured plan in this format for each of the 5 pillars:

---
PILLAR [N]: [Pillar Name]
Head Keyword: [primary keyword]
Pillar Page Title: [recommended title]
Word Count Target: 4,000 words

Subpillar Articles:
1. [Title] | Keyword: [kw] | Intent: [intent] | Words: [count]
2. [Title] | Keyword: [kw] | Intent: [intent] | Words: [count]
... (10 total)
---

## Content Calendar Recommendation

Build out pillars in this order:
1. Create all 5 pillar hub pages first (establishes topical breadth)
2. Fill in subpillars for your strongest/most competitive pillar
3. Publish 2-3 subpillars per week to signal consistent topical investment
4. Interlink as you go — never publish an article without internal links
